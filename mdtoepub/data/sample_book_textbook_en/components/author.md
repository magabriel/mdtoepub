# About the author

Clara M. Rivers is an educational psychologist and learning specialist with over fifteen years of experience in cognitive science and study methodology. She holds a PhD from the University of Cambridge and has published extensively on memory, attention, and skill acquisition.

Her workshops on effective learning strategies have been attended by thousands of students and professionals across the English-speaking world. She is known for her ability to translate complex scientific findings into practical, actionable advice.

*The Art of Learning* is her most ambitious work to date, synthesising decades of research into a comprehensive guide for anyone who wishes to learn more effectively.

She currently lives in Cambridge, where she divides her time between research, teaching, and writing.
