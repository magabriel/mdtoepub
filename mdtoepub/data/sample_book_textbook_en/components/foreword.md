When Clara Rivers first showed me the manuscript of *The Art of Learning*, I was sceptical. Another book on study techniques, I thought. Another collection of tips and tricks dressed up in scientific jargon.

I was wrong.

What Rivers has produced is something genuinely rare: a book that is at once deeply researched and eminently practical, grounded in the latest cognitive science yet written with the clarity and warmth of a gifted teacher. This is not a book you read and forget. It is a book that changes the way you think about thinking.

I have spent thirty years in education, and I can count on one hand the works that have genuinely influenced my practice. *The Art of Learning* is one of them.

Professor Sir Michael Bentley
Institute of Education
University of London
