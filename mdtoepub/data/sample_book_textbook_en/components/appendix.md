# Resources and Recommended Reading

## Books

- Dweck, C. S. (2006). *Mindset: The New Psychology of Success*. Random House.
- Brown, P. C., Roediger, H. L., and McDaniel, M. A. (2014). *Make It Stick: The Science of Successful Learning*. Harvard University Press.
- Oakley, B. (2014). *A Mind for Numbers: How to Excel at Math and Science*. TarcherPerigee.
- Newport, C. (2016). *Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World*. Grand Central Publishing.
- Cirillo, F. (2006). *The Pomodoro Technique*. Creative Commons.

## Academic articles

- Simons, D. J., and Chabris, C. F. (1999). Gorillas in our midst: Sustained inattentional blindness for dynamic events. *Perception*, 28(9), 1059-1074.
- Roediger, H. L., and Karpicke, J. D. (2006). Test-enhanced learning: Taking memory tests improves long-term retention. *Psychological Science*, 17(3), 249-255.
- Ebbinghaus, H. (1885). *Uber das Gedachtnis*. Leipzig: Duncker and Humblot.
- Vygotsky, L. S. (1978). *Mind in Society*. Harvard University Press.

## Online resources

- **Coursera:** "Learning How to Learn" by Barbara Oakley and Terrence Sejnowski
- **Anki:** Free spaced repetition software (https://apps.ankiweb.net)
- **Forest:** A focus app that uses the Pomodoro principle
- **Notion:** An excellent tool for keeping a learning journal
