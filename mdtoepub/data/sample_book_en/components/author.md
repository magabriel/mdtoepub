# About the author

Elena M. Vasquez was born in Lisbon in 1978. She is a historian and writer, specializing in the study of nineteenth-century British documentary archives. She has published numerous academic articles on the preservation of historical heritage at universities in London, Oxford, and Salamanca.

*The Vestige of Centuries* is her first novel, the fruit of years of research in the municipal archives of southern England. In it, she weaves her knowledge of the era with a mystery plot that explores the boundaries between official history and the stories silenced by time.

She currently resides in Bath, where she continues to research and write.
