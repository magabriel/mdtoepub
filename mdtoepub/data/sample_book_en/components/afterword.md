# Afterword

This novel is inspired by real events and people, although the main plot is fiction. The National Archives at Kew holds in its collections an uncatalogued file, dated 1888, which mentions the disappearance of a researcher and the loss of an ancient book. The specific details of the case have been lost, but the file exists.

The star map alluded to in the novel is based on a manuscript held in the Cambridge University Library, discovered by the author during a research visit in 2018.

The rest, as they say, is silence.

Bath, spring of 2025
